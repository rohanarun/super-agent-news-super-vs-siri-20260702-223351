Why computer use matters now

Local context vs execution

Siri’s strength is understanding what’s already on your device. In hands‑on testing, it outperformed Gemini at pulling specific details from private messages and emails and acting on them quickly.

Durability beats novelty

One‑off assistance is table stakes. Teams increasingly want agents that can repeat the same computer tasks reliably without paying full cost every time.
